13 MAGNIFICENT GALAXIES

Throughout the ages, we have looked to the night sky in awe, wonderment and signs of life.

When lack of light allows and darkness prevails, the night sky lights up with thousands of shining faces, smiles that seem like mere stardust sprinkled across the slate of our eyes perception.

Every dot of light containing burning life, blazing millions of light years away. Clusters of floating fireflies. Massive giants of spiraling stars, suns, planets, flying rocks and an entity grasping onto them, embracing it's body, nurturing its young.

These are the melody's of stardust, these are the galaxies.
